8 Playbooks
Compliance & Regulatory
Automates response to regulatory demands across every major framework — SEC, FINRA, HIPAA, DOJ, FTC, EEOC, FOIA, GDPR, CCPA, and subpoenas — collecting data, applying exemptions, flagging privilege, and assembling production-ready packages with a defensible audit trail, in hours rather than weeks.
01
SEC / FINRA Audit Response
When the SEC or FINRA issues a formal demand for trader communications — emails, Bloomberg chats, instant messages — across dozens of custodians, the platform identifies every relevant individual, collects data simultaneously across all connected sources, and assembles a fully indexed, regulator-ready production package with a complete chain-of-custody log.
30-day SEC windows met without weeks of manual collection.
02
HIPAA / OCR Investigation
Correlates access logs from EHR systems with email metadata and network activity to automatically construct a forensic timeline of every interaction with a patient record. Deviations from the hospital's access policies are highlighted with confidence scores, and the full evidence set is packaged for HHS Office for Civil Rights review.
Defensible patient data timelines without manual cross-system correlation.
03
DOJ Antitrust / CID Response
Runs concept-based searches to surface pricing strategy discussions, competitor references, and coordination signals that keyword searches miss. Correlates communication timestamps with known market events and assembles a fully documented Civil Investigative Demand response with privilege log, before the DOJ deadline.
Catches collusion signals even when parties deliberately avoid obvious terminology.
04
FTC Investigation Response
When the Federal Trade Commission investigates consumer protection or unfair trade practice allegations, the platform collects all relevant communications and business records across custodians, applies concept-based review to surface responsive content, and assembles a production package that meets FTC Civil Investigative Demand specifications.
FTC demands met with the same speed and defensibility as DOJ responses.
05
EEOC / Employment Regulatory Response
Responds to EEOC charges and state labor board investigations by collecting all relevant HR communications, performance records, and employment documentation across the organization. Privilege is applied to attorney-client discussions, and a structured response package is generated — including a chronological timeline of key employment events relevant to the charge.
Employment investigations that don't expose privileged HR strategy.
06
FOIA Request Response
Ingests each Freedom of Information Act request, parses scope using NLP, searches all agency repositories simultaneously, and auto-applies correct statutory exemption tags — b(3), b(5), b(6), b(7) — with documented legal justification for each. Redacted documents and a full redaction log are packaged automatically within the 20-business-day statutory window.
200+ FOIA requests a quarter — every deadline met, every exemption documented.
07
Data Subject Rights Manager
Handles the full spectrum of data subject rights requests — access, deletion, portability, rectification, and opt-out — regardless of the regulation triggering them (GDPR, CCPA, PIPEDA, LGPD, and others). Searches all connected data stores by subject identity, compiles a complete record inventory, applies the correct jurisdiction-specific exemptions, and auto-generates a compliant response package. Deletion requests trigger automated cross-system workflows with a confirmation audit trail.
Every data subject right, every regulation, every deadline — handled from one place.
08
Subpoena Response
Parses third-party subpoenas using NLP to extract custodians, date ranges, and subject matter scope. Automatically collects all relevant data, classifies documents as responsive or non-responsive, applies privilege review, and packages the final production — complete with cover letter, privilege log, and certificate of completeness — within the required deadline.
21-day subpoena deadlines met without burning attorney hours on manual scoping.
4 Playbooks
HR & Employee Investigations
When misconduct is alleged, IP walks out the door, or a senior employee resigns unexpectedly, HR and legal need to move fast, quietly, and with a defensible evidence trail. These playbooks cover the full spectrum — from harassment investigations to forensic exit management.
09
Workplace Misconduct Investigation
Silently collects all communications between named parties across email, Slack, Teams, and any other connected channel — without alerting those individuals. Messages are clustered by topic and scored by sentiment so investigators immediately see the most relevant exchanges and escalating patterns.
Confidential investigations that surface what matters without reading thousands of messages.
10
Employee Termination & Data Theft
Reconstructs a departing employee's complete data activity trail — every file opened, document emailed externally, USB device connected, cloud upload — for any pre-departure period. Generates a risk-scored evidence package quantifying IP exposure, ready for cease-and-desist or litigation.
Know precisely what IP left the building before the employee does.
11
Whistleblower Investigation
Runs concept-based searches across email, financial systems, and collaboration tools to surface anomalies in language, approvals, and payment patterns — even when deliberate obfuscation is used. Automatically builds an entity relationship graph linking subjects to vendors, approvals, and transactions.
A structured map of the alleged scheme, not an unorganized pile of documents.
12
Employee Exit Management
On resignation, automatically checks the employee against all active legal holds, assesses data access seniority, assigns a risk tier, and issues a tailored exit questionnaire. Responses are analyzed for signals of concern and the platform produces a recommended action plan before the last day of employment.
The data window closes when the employee leaves — this closes it first.
3 Playbooks
Information Governance
Data you don't need is a liability. Data you can't find is a risk. These playbooks help organizations defensibly delete what they shouldn't keep, enforce what regulations require them to retain, and surface sensitive data buried in legacy systems before it causes problems.
13
ROT Data Cleanup
Uses a three-phase funnel — surface scan, metadata scan, content scan — so full analysis runs only on high-probability candidates, not entire repositories. Items cleared for deletion receive a defensible certificate with a complete audit trail. Legal holds and retention obligations are automatically honored throughout.
Defensible deletion at scale — without the legal exposure of blind mass deletion.
14
Records Retention Enforcement
Maps all active communication channels — email, Teams, WhatsApp, mobile archivers — and immediately flags any not yet captured. Uses a three-phase funnel to assign retention categories across the full population. Compliance officers receive real-time alerts the moment any channel shows a capture gap or a record nears premature deletion.
Real-time visibility into every channel — and every gap — before regulators find them.
15
Dark Data Discovery
A three-phase funnel inventories and risk-rates entire repositories before opening a single document. Full content analysis — PII detection, IP classification, regulated-data recognition — runs only on confirmed high-risk items. Also serves as a data quality checkpoint before feeding enterprise data into AI models, removing duplicates, stale records, and sensitive content that should never enter a training pipeline.
Know what's in your data before migrating it — or before feeding it to AI.
2 Playbooks
M&A
Due diligence windows are tight and the cost of missing a liability is enormous. These playbooks compress weeks of contract review into days, and surface every data governance gap in a newly acquired entity before it becomes the acquirer's problem.
16
M&A Due Diligence
Ingests all target company documents and automatically extracts key contract terms — auto-renewal clauses, change-of-control provisions, liability caps, IP ownership, non-compete terms. Unusual or high-risk clauses are flagged for attorney review. Litigation history is cross-referenced against contract obligations, and a structured risk summary is generated.
Five years of contracts reviewed in days — not the three weeks you don't have.
17
Post-Merger Data Integration Audit
Conducts a complete data audit of the acquired entity's systems, mapping all existing data against the acquiring company's retention policies, legal hold obligations, and regulatory requirements. Surfaces every gap — records that should be on hold but aren't, data retained past schedule, unlicensed third-party data use — and produces a prioritized remediation plan.
Surface every liability you just acquired before it surfaces itself.
2 Playbooks
Cybersecurity
Threats from inside and outside the organization demand different responses — but both require the same thing: a complete, correlated picture of what happened, what data was involved, and what the legal obligations are. These playbooks deliver that picture before the clock runs out.
18
Insider Threat Detection
Ingests telemetry from all connected security and communication systems, establishes a behavioral baseline based on historical activity, and scores deviations in real time. When anomaly thresholds are crossed, it automatically assembles a complete evidence bundle — emails, file access logs, DLP events, and timeline — and escalates to legal and security with a risk rating.
The full picture assembled automatically — before the damage is done.
19
Breach Incident Response
Automatically triages all affected systems, identifies which data stores were accessed during the attack window, classifies data types involved (PII, health records, financial data), maps impacted subjects by jurisdiction, and drafts jurisdiction-specific breach notification reports — with a complete forensic package ready for regulators and outside counsel within the notification window.
GDPR's 72-hour clock starts ticking the moment you detect a breach. Be ready.
2 Playbooks
IP & Contracts
Intellectual property theft and missed contract obligations are among the most financially damaging — and most preventable — risks an organization faces. These playbooks build court-ready IP cases automatically and ensure no renewal or obligation ever slips through unnoticed.
20
IP Theft Investigation
Automatically correlates every relevant data source — DLP alerts, email logs, access records, file hash comparisons — and reconstructs the complete exfiltration sequence with precise timestamps. Calculates the scope and estimated value of stolen IP, identifies whether data was forwarded or stored elsewhere, and generates a court-ready forensic report with full chain-of-custody documentation.
A court-ready forensic case, built automatically, before counsel is even briefed.
21
Contract Obligation Tracking
Ingests all contracts regardless of format or storage location, extracts key dates, renewal terms, and commercial obligations using NLP, and normalizes everything into a structured, searchable tracker. Contract owners are automatically alerted 90, 60, and 30 days before critical deadlines — with a plain-language summary of what action is required and what is at stake.
Never miss an auto-renewal again across 800+ active agreements.
7 Playbooks
Litigation
From the moment a complaint lands to the day a matter closes, litigation generates enormous data demands and enormous cost. These playbooks eliminate spoliation risk, reduce review spend by 60–80%, and give legal leadership the analytical foundation to make smarter decisions at every stage.
22
Litigation Hold Management
Automatically identifies all relevant custodians, issues jurisdiction-appropriate legal hold notices, and tracks acknowledgment in real time. Non-responders trigger automatic escalation to managers. Maintains a continuously updated, defensible audit trail showing every notice sent, acknowledgment received, and escalation triggered throughout the matter's lifetime.
Every notice tracked, every acknowledgment logged, zero spoliation risk.
23
Early Case Assessment (ECA)
Collects all communications and documents related to the dispute, identifies key factual issues, and evaluates claim strength against the internal documentary record. Estimates the cost of full review if the matter proceeds, models probable discovery scope, and produces a structured decision memo — giving leadership the information needed to choose the right strategy before committing to litigation.
Settle, fight, or counter — with the facts, not assumptions.
24
eDiscovery Collection & Preservation Tracking
Creates a structured preservation and collection request for each identified data source, assigns it to the relevant data steward with clear instructions and deadlines, and tracks completion in real time. Every steward action is timestamped in an unalterable audit trail, maintaining a defensible chain of custody even without automated connectors. Escalations trigger automatically for overdue responses.
Defensible chain of custody — even when there is no connector.
25
Document Review & Categorization
Runs AI-assisted first-pass across the entire document population using concept-based search, clustering, and continuous active learning. Near-duplicate documents are batched so one attorney decision applies to thousands of similar records. Review volume is reduced by 60–80% before any attorney opens a document. Privilege logs are auto-generated as a natural output of review — not a separate exercise.
2.4 million documents. A fraction of the cost. A fraction of the time.
26
Deposition & Witness Preparation
Builds a witness-specific document profile for each deponent — surfacing all communications authored or received by the individual, flagging where their statements conflict with the documentary record, and identifying the highest-risk exhibits by content and context. Counsel receives a structured preparation package per witness, including a key event timeline and a ranked list of documents likely to be used by opposing counsel.
No witness walks into deposition blindsided by their own documents.
27
Cross-Matter Analytics & Spend Management
Aggregates data across all active matters, maps custodian and collection overlap between cases, and identifies where document sets already collected can be reused — eliminating redundant collection costs. Provides a real-time dashboard of spend by matter, firm, phase, and task code. Flags billing anomalies and guideline deviations for GC review.
47 matters. 8 firms. One view — and no duplicate collection spend.
Settlement & Damages Analysis
Integrates claim data, product records, financial exposure calculations, and prior settlement comparables to build a dynamic damages model across all claimants. Models multiple settlement scenarios by participation rate, claim type, and jurisdiction. Identifies the documentary record most relevant to the mediator's likely focus areas.
Walk into mediation with a data-driven exposure range — not instinct and spreadsheets.
